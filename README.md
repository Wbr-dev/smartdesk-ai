# SmartDesk AI

> AI-powered Multi-Tenant SaaS platform for Intelligent IT Service Management (ITSM).

---

## 📖 Overview

SmartDesk AI is a modern IT Service Management (ITSM) platform designed to centralize IT support operations, automate repetitive tasks through Artificial Intelligence, and deliver a scalable, secure and user-friendly experience.

The platform follows a Multi-Tenant SaaS architecture, allowing multiple companies to use the same application while keeping their data isolated.

---

## 🎯 Mission

Simplify IT service management through automation, organization and Artificial Intelligence.

---

## 🚀 Vision

Become a modern platform capable of helping organizations deliver faster, safer and more intelligent IT services.
